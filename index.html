OEM & Pricing

The price advantage, in numbers

OEM cost is ~20% of MSRP. SEOULIVA supplies at ~30% of MSRP, so the buyer keeps ~70%.

How every $1 of MSRP breaks down

Illustrative structure at ~30% supply price.

What this means for the buyer

  • ~70% gross margin headroom for warehouse discounting and resale.
  • OEM cost ≈ 20% of MSRP — sustainable economics.
  • Customisable formula, fragrance, packaging and labelling.
  • Scalable MOQs — start lean, expand as sell-through is proven.
